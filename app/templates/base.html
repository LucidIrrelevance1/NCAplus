FedEx Hub

This has been made to simplify the creation of VLANs and their access to the ACI fabric. It is able to create groups, VLANs, virtual port channels, VLAN profiles and provide access to the ACI with just few clicks. Besides, it is able to send commands to other devices that are not part of the ACI fabric.
